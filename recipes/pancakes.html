Pancakes

Description

It's a family recipe. So can assure that these pancakes are great. Try it yourself. You need very few ingredient and it doesn't take much time. This recipe is for 4 Persons.

Ingredients

  • 200g flour
  • 1/2l milk
  • 12 Eggs
  • 1 spoon of butter for the pan

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients
  2. Put dough in pan and make pancakes
