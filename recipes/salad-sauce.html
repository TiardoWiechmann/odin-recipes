Salad-sauce

Description

This salad-sauce is perfect for every green salad. It consists of healthy ingredient like apple vinegar, mustard and olive oil. Of course this sauce is very delicious as well.

Ingredients

  • 1/8 l olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon balsamic creme
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • 1 teaspoon mustard
  • 1 pressed clove of garlic
  • 1/2 onion diced
  • herbal salt, pepper and perhaps chives

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a glass
  2. Let the sauce steep vor one day
  3. Use the sauce only for the portion you are going to eat to avoid having a soaked salad after some time
